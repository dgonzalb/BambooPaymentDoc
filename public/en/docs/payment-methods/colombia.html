Colombia

A diverse country with region-specific consumption preferences, Colombians are early adopters of technology solutions while still clinging to traditional habits. Colombia is one of the startup hubs of Latin America, and new electronic payment methods, including digital wallets, coexist with a diverse list of cash payment solutions.

This section shows the available payment methods, currencies, and related information you must consider when processing in Colombia.
