Uruguay

Uruguay has a developing e-commerce industry that is progressively gaining favor with customers. A favorable regulatory framework, rising smartphone usage, and increased internet penetration contributed to Uruguay’s booming e-commerce sector.

This section shows the available payment methods, currencies, and related information you must consider when processing in Uruguay.
