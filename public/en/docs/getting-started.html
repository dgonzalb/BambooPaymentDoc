Getting started

This section exposes the Bamboo Payment API functionalities for secure integration with our platform.

Overview

Using our platform, you can offer your customers an improved payment experience, being able to request card details as if they were on your page or mobile application through our Checkout Form.

You can also request the data only once and store a token associated with the card the customer entered securely on our platform.

PCI-DSS

Our certified PCI-DSS Level 1 platform allows us to process card numbers and other sensitive customer authentication data.

You can embed the Checkout Form in your pages or mobile applications; you can now accept payments without receiving confidential information such as customer data, provide security to your customers, and ease of use.

Start processing with Bamboo.

Follow these steps to start processing payments with Bamboo Payment:

  1. Sign Up.
    Create your merchant account by contacting our sales representatives.

  2. Get Credentials.
    After we activate your account, you will receive the API Keys credentials to access the STAGE Environment.

  3. Integrate the APIs.
    Follow the instructions in this documentation to integrate our payment APIs. You must follow the homologation process to move your account from STAGE to PRODUCTION.

  4. Go Live.
    Once you have completed the tests required in step 3, request the Go Live with our support team.

  5. Process payments.
    Once you have your production credentials, you can switch to the PRODUCTION environment and start processing your payments!

Don't get stuck!

If you have any technical doubts or require further information, please feel free to contact our support team for assistance.
